Welcome to BasementJam

Formed in 2023 in an underground rehearsal room in Stuttgart Ost, BasementJam is a four-piece band blending pop, rock, indie, urban blues, and UK hip-hop influences. BasementJam does covers and original music that hooks you instantly, and stays with you long after.
